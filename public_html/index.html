Email list

To join our email list, you have two options:

  • Fill out our online form, or
  • Send an e-mail, with only the word "subscribe" in the body of the message (leave the subject blank), to annarbor-pm-request@pm.org

You can then send email to the group at annarbor-pm@pm.org.

Visit our email list archive to view past messages.

About

We're an easy going group of developers, designers, hackers, entrepreneurs & day-dreamers. We enjoy Perl, but we encourage conversation and presentations from other languages.

We are pretty informal, and only hold meetings when someone brings it up on the mailing list. Want to present something? Join the mailing list and propose a meeting.
